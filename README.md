Executive Summary:
This project aims to develop a School Admission Portal for Lahore Grammar School (LGS) to provide accurate and accessible information for students, parents, and teachers. The website provides details on the admission process, school information, and academic offerings in a well-structured and user-friendly way.

Key features of the website include:

Simple navigation with a clean, easy-to-use interface
Pages providing school information, admission process, and contact details
A blue-and-white theme reflecting the school's identity
Responsive design for both desktop and mobile devices
The target audience of this portal includes prospective students, parents, and staff members.

Site Structure & Navigation:
The website is structured into several pages to ensure clear navigation and easy access to important information. The main pages of the portal are as follows:

1. Home Page
Overview of the school, key announcements, and quick links to other sections.
2. About Us
Provides details about the mission, vision, and values of LGS.
3. Admissions
Information on admission criteria, process, and important dates for new students.
4. Contact Us
Contact details for the school, including address, phone number, email, and a contact form.
5. Photo Gallery
Images of school events, activities, and campus to give visitors a visual insight into school life.
6. Scholarship
Different scholarships are given to students.
Fonts:
Arial: Chosen for simplicity, readability, and consistency across all pages.
Layout:
A clean and responsive layout that works well on both desktop and mobile devices.

Introduction to Lahore Grammar School (LGS):
Lahore Grammar School is one of the leading private educational institutions in Pakistan. Known for its high academic standards, LGS offers a strong educational foundation from early childhood to pre-university levels.

LGS emphasizes academic excellence, confidence-building, discipline, and character development, preparing students for success in national and international environments.

Admission Criteria:
The admission process at LGS includes:

Age eligibility for the class applied.
Previous academic performance evaluation.
Written admission test to assess student capabilities.
Interview with the student and parents.
Admission Procedure:
The steps to apply for admission at LGS are as follows:

Collect the admission form from the school campus.
Complete the form with accurate details.
Submit the required documents.
Appear for the admission test.
Interview with the student and parents.
Final confirmation of admission.
